History

{{ name }} started with many requests from family and friends wanting Mrs. Hunt to coordinate important events. It started with small events and eventually morphed into weddings and retirements ceremonies. Most of the reviews received emphasized quality and cost. As one client stated, "I can't believe the quality of service I received for the cost."
Mrs. Hunt's inspiration comes from the latest trends, customer's visions and her desire to make each event unique and catered to each client's wants and desires. The name, Rowland Hills, derived from her father whom she never met. Mrs. Hunt inherited his passion for planning and catering as he had the opportunity to cater for former President Jimmy Carter and State Troopers.
